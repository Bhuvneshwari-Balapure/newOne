About Us

Welcome to Anant Enterprise, your trusted source for high-quality nylon cloth.

Founded by Shashank Gupta, we are a proprietorship based in Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to providing exceptional textile products that meet the needs of our diverse clientele.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to excellence and customer satisfaction. Our principal location is situated at Inta Rori, near Masjid, Sath Devi Mandir, providing easy access to our products and services. We are registered under the regular type of registration, with our certificate issued on December 30, 2021.

At Anant Enterprise, we pride ourselves on our integrity, quality, and reliability. Whether you're in need of nylon cloth for industrial, commercial, or personal use, we are here to serve you. Explore our offerings and discover how we can meet your textile needs.

For more information, please feel free to reach out to us.
